This is a demo project. Meridian Trust is not a real bank. This privacy policy is written to reflect what a real banking privacy policy would look like, but no actual personal data is collected, stored, or processed.

1. Information we collect

When you open an account or use our services, we collect information necessary to provide banking services and comply with applicable law.

Information you give us

  • Identity information — your name, date of birth, and government-issued ID details
  • Contact information — email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Account credentials — your username and encrypted password
  • Financial information — linked external accounts, transaction details, and funding sources

Information we collect automatically

  • Device and browser information used to access your account
  • IP addresses and approximate location data
  • Login timestamps and session activity for security monitoring
  • Transaction metadata such as timestamps, amounts, and merchant categories

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve our services — and for nothing else without your consent.

  • Opening and managing your accounts and processing transactions
  • Verifying your identity and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations, including anti-money laundering requirements
  • Sending you account alerts, statements, and security notifications
  • Responding to your support requests
  • Improving and personalising our products and services

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers. We do not use your financial data to serve you ads.

3. Sharing your data

We share your information only in limited circumstances.

Service providers

We work with third-party vendors who help us deliver our services — for example, cloud infrastructure providers and payment processors. These vendors are contractually prohibited from using your data for any purpose beyond providing their service to us.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information when required to do so by law, court order, or regulatory authority — including responses to lawful subpoenas and requests from financial regulators.

Business transfers

If Meridian Trust is acquired or merges with another entity, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Security

We apply industry-standard protections to your data at every layer.

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we have no ability to retrieve your plaintext password
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • We monitor for suspicious login activity and will alert you if we detect anything unusual
  • Our infrastructure is regularly audited for vulnerabilities

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

5. Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies — only what is necessary to operate the service.

  • Session cookies to keep you signed in while you use the app
  • Security cookies to detect and prevent fraudulent login attempts
  • Preference cookies to remember settings like language and timezone

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that share your data with outside companies.

6. Your rights

You have meaningful control over your personal data.

  • Access — you can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — you can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — you can request that we delete your account and associated data
  • Portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — you can object to certain uses of your data where we rely on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and for a period afterward as required by law. Banking regulations typically require us to retain transaction records for a minimum of seven years after account closure.

When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymise it so it can no longer be linked to you.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email at least 30 days before they take effect and post the updated policy here with a new effective date.

Continuing to use our services after a policy change takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or want to exercise your data rights, reach out to our privacy team.
