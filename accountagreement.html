This is a demo project. Meridian Trust is not a real bank and this agreement has no legal force. It is written to illustrate what a real account agreement looks like.

1. Eligibility

To open and maintain an account with Meridian Trust, you must meet the following requirements at all times.

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be a resident of the United States with a valid Social Security Number or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number
  • Provide accurate and complete identity information as required by federal law
  • Not have had an account with Meridian Trust involuntarily closed within the past five years

By opening an account, you represent that you meet all eligibility requirements and agree to notify us immediately if your circumstances change.

2. Account types

Meridian Trust offers three account types. Each is governed by this agreement plus the specific terms disclosed at account opening.

Everyday Checking

A demand deposit account intended for day-to-day transactions. No minimum balance required. No monthly maintenance fee. Comes with a Visa debit card linked to the account.

High-Yield Savings

An interest-bearing account. The Annual Percentage Yield (APY) is variable and may change at any time. Interest is calculated daily and credited monthly. No minimum balance to earn interest.

12-Month Certificate of Deposit (CD)

A fixed-term deposit account. Your rate is locked for the full 12-month term. Early withdrawal is permitted subject to an early withdrawal penalty equal to 90 days of interest on the amount withdrawn.

3. Deposits

You may fund your account by the following methods.

  • ACH transfer from a linked external bank account
  • Mobile check deposit via the Meridian Trust app
  • Incoming wire transfer
  • Direct deposit from an employer or government agency

Funds availability

ACH transfers from verified external accounts are generally available within one business day. Mobile check deposits are subject to a standard hold period of one to two business days. Wire transfers are available the same business day when received before 4:00 PM ET.

We reserve the right to place extended holds on deposits in cases of suspected fraud, new accounts, or deposits that exceed normal patterns.

4. Withdrawals and transfers

You may withdraw or transfer funds from your checking or savings account at any time, subject to available balance. CD accounts are subject to the early withdrawal penalty described in Section 2.

Transfer limits

Outbound ACH transfers are subject to a default daily limit of $10,000. Wire transfers are subject to a default daily limit of $25,000. You may request a limit increase by contacting support.

Overdrafts

We do not charge overdraft fees. If a transaction would overdraw your checking account, it will be declined. If your account balance falls below zero due to a pending transaction, you have two business days to bring your balance to zero before the account is suspended.

6. Debit cards

Your Everyday Checking account comes with a Visa debit card. The card may be used for purchases anywhere Visa is accepted and for ATM withdrawals worldwide.

  • You are responsible for all transactions made with your card or card number
  • Report a lost or stolen card immediately through the app or by calling us — your liability for unauthorised transactions is limited to $50 if reported within two business days
  • You may freeze your card instantly at any time through the app at no charge
  • Replacement cards are free and arrive within 5 business days; expedited delivery is available for $15

7. Electronic access and security

Access to your account online or through the Meridian Trust app is governed by our Electronic Services Agreement, which is incorporated into this agreement by reference.

Your responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials confidential and do not share them with anyone
  • Use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication
  • Notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access to your account
  • Review your account statements regularly and report any errors within 60 days

Our responsibilities

We will take reasonable steps to secure your account and notify you of any security incidents that affect your data. We will never ask for your password by email, phone, or text message.

8. Closing an account

You may close your account at any time by contacting us. There is no fee for closing an account. Any remaining balance will be transferred to a linked external account or issued as a check within five business days.

We may close your account at any time with at least 30 days notice, or immediately without notice if we determine that the account has been used for fraudulent or illegal activity, or if you have materially breached this agreement.

Upon closure, outstanding debit card transactions that have not yet settled will still be deducted from your final balance.

9. Disputes

Transaction disputes

If you believe a transaction on your account is unauthorised or in error, contact us within 60 days of the statement date on which the transaction appeared. We will investigate and respond within 10 business days.

Arbitration

Any dispute arising from this agreement that cannot be resolved informally will be settled by binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association. You waive the right to participate in a class action lawsuit related to your account.

This arbitration clause does not apply to claims that qualify for small claims court.

10. Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Illinois and applicable federal law. If any provision of this agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions will remain in full effect.

We may amend this agreement at any time. We will provide at least 30 days written notice of material changes. Continued use of your account after the effective date of a change constitutes your acceptance of the amended agreement.
